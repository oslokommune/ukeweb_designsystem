Having the option to trigger content visible is a good way to compress the length of the page. Content expanded by default are more often read than content collapsed.

Important

Triggers get the .osg-collapsible-trigger--expanded modifier when content is expanded.
Triggers should be wrapper in heading elements where possible to help screen readers

Spacing classes are available in both margin and padding, from 0 to 25 (see sizes below), at all four sides and as horizontal (left|right) and vertical (top|bottom) groups. Additionally each class is available for the breakpoints small (default), medium and large.

Sizes

none = 0px 5 = 5px 10 = 10px 15 = 15px 20 = 20px 30 = 30px 40 = 40px 50 = 50px 60 = 60px 75 = 75px 100 = 100px

Classes

.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-none{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-5{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-10{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-15{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-20{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-30{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-40{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-50{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-60{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-75{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-100{-breakpoint-medium|large}
